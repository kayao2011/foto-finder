About Us

Who are we?

Well, we're a charity run by young people (20 somethings to be a little more precise), for young people. We've partnered with London's most influencial creative and political organisations including the University of the Arts (UAL) to bring you the best opportunities and advice on how to kick start your creative career.

Our goal

Is simple: We aim to connect young people in London with creative opportunities in the city. Ultimately, we want to increase our generation's political voice and influence across all creative industries.

What we do

There are 3 activities we do to help you:

1) CV/application workshops,

2) Maintain an opporunities board,

3) Provide creative career advice an support.
